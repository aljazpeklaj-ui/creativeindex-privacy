1. Introduction

CreativeIndex ("we," "our," or "us") operates an ad intelligence platform that helps marketers discover high-performing advertising creatives and analyze competitor strategies. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 User Account Information

  • Email address and profile information from authentication providers (LinkedIn, Replit)
  • Account preferences and settings
  • Usage analytics and platform interaction data

2.2 Advertising Data via APIs

  • Public advertising content from Meta Ad Library, LinkedIn Marketing API, and Reddit Ads API
  • Campaign performance metrics and targeting information
  • Creative assets (images, videos, ad copy) for analysis purposes
  • This data is collected from public advertising libraries and partner APIs for intelligence purposes

2.3 User-Generated Content

  • Saved creative collections and templates
  • Team collaboration data and shared content
  • Feedback and support communications

2.4 Technical Information

  • IP address and browser information
  • Device type and operating system
  • Cookies and local storage data
  • Usage patterns and feature interactions

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Provision

  • Provide ad intelligence and creative discovery features
  • Enable team collaboration and content sharing
  • Generate performance insights and competitive analysis
  • Improve our platform's functionality and user experience

3.2 Communication

  • Send important service updates and security notifications
  • Respond to support requests and feedback
  • Provide optional marketing communications (with consent)

3.3 Legal and Security

  • Comply with legal obligations and platform policies
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Maintain your login session and preferences
  • Analyze platform usage and performance
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse

You can control cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that disabling certain cookies may limit platform functionality.

We may disclose information when required by law, regulation, or legal process.

If CreativeIndex is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

5.5 Aggregated Data

We may share anonymized, aggregated insights about advertising trends that cannot identify individual users or specific campaigns.

6. Data Storage and Security

6.1 Data Location

Your data is stored securely on cloud infrastructure with industry-standard security measures.

6.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Access controls and authentication requirements
  • Secure API integrations with partner platforms
  • Regular security assessments and penetration testing

6.3 Data Retention

  • User account data: Retained while your account is active
  • Advertising intelligence data: Retained for analytical purposes, with periodic cleanup of outdated content
  • Support communications: Retained for reasonable periods to provide ongoing support
  • Analytics data: Typically retained for up to 2 years for platform improvement

6.4 Data Breach Procedures

In the event of a data breach that may affect your personal information, we will notify you and relevant authorities as required by applicable law, typically within 72 hours of discovery.

7.4 GDPR Rights (EU Residents)

If you are a resident of the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR:

  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Right to restrict processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw consent for data processing

7.5 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

California residents have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected and used
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights
